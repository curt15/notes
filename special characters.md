--- 
newline character: **\\n**
```py
>>> s = 'my string'
>>> s += '\npart 2'
>>> s
'my string\npart 2'
>>> print(s)
my string
part 2
```

tab: **\\t**
```py
>>> s = 'to do:'
>>> s += '\n\t- grocery shopping'
>>> s
'to do:\n\t- grocery shopping'
>>> print(s)
to do:
	- grocery shopping
```
